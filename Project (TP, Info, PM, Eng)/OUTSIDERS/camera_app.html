THE OUTSIDERS

Susan Eloise Hinton

April 20, 2006

Introduction

The Outsiders talks about the life of a 14-year-old boy called Ponyboy. He and his friends are greasers, young members of a gang on the East Side, the poor side of town. The greasers' rivals are the Socs, short for Socials, who are the "West-side rich kids."
